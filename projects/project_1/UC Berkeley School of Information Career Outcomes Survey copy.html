Thank you for completing this survey! 

I School Career Services is here to help with your job search or career concerns. MIDS graduates are eligible to utilize career services and resources including:
 
Career Advising - schedule individual appointments online for assistance with all aspects of the job search.
Resume, Cover Letter, and LinkedIn Profile Reviews - complete this form and upload your documents to get feedback via email, with no appointment needed. 
I School Job Board -  peruse job postings from employers who want to hire I School students and graduates. You can set up an email alert for convenient access to job openings.
MIDS Resume Book - submit your resume to gain exposure to employers throughout the year. 
Career Webinars - check our events calendar for upcoming live webinars or watch recordings of past webinars at your convenience.
Online Career Resources - explore our curated collection of links to access career tips and resources 24/7.
Slack - connect with the #alumni and #mids-alumni channels on Slack, our official community platform.  To help build your network, update your profile with your alumni status and employment information (click on your profile picture in the top right of Slack to get started).

To access all of our services and resources, go to: https://www.ischool.berkeley.edu/careers/students

Is your organization hiring data science talent? To learn how we can help with your recruiting needs, go to: https://www.ischool.berkeley.edu/careers/employers

Feel free to contact us with questions or for assistance at any time by emailing mids_careerservices@ischool.berkeley.edu.
